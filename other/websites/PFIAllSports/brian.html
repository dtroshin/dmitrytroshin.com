Brian Ackley is a writer, director, producer, and editor for One Way Or Another Productions, a film company with more than 10 films in its current library. In 2008 Brian directed one feature film and co-produced two others. Brian has worked as a freelance videographer and editor for a number of independent clients. Since 2002, Brian has been associated with Beacon 43, an after school program administered by F.E.G.S. Far Rockaway in NY. His responsibilities have ranged from office management to video production instructor to activity specialist. Before that, Brian was the Office Manager for A.R. Real Estate Management based in Brooklyn.

Brian is the Executive Associate Director of PFI All-Sports. He supervises the design and maintenance of the infrastructure, accounting and payroll, and manages expenses and purchases.
