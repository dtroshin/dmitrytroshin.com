PFI ALL-SPORTS - MANAGEMENT

RENE LEONARD

Rene Leonard is an experienced photojournalist, videographer and editor whose worked with such companies as Rueters and AFP (Agence France Presse), the biggest competitors of Associated Press. He has previously been published with National Geographic Magazine and has spent a number of years filming in diverse locations within the Caribbean and Central America. In 2002, Rene co-founded JAGA Productions wherein he currently holds positions as DP (Director of Photography) and Co-Producer. Rene currently works as a sports photographer for News 12. He has a degree in Film Production and Film Studies.

Rene is the Director of PFI All-Sports. He oversees the design and maintenance of the website, advertising and cross-marketing, sales, and research and development for the company.

TONY JEAN

Tony Jean is an experienced photojournalist, videographer and editor who has been with News 12 Long Island since 2005. He has worked freelance as a producer and director of live concerts, sporting events, and taped interviews since 2004. Though trained as a cameraman and editor (Avid and Final Cut Pro), Tony also has experience with lighting, audio board operation, crane and boom operation, on air reporting, VCR, and teleprompter operation. Tony is a member of Make a Wish Foundation/ United Way and of Ebenezer Baptist Church of Westbury where he offers his talents to tape services and events as needed. Tony has his degree in Communication Art.

Tony is the Production Manager of PFI All-Sports. He is responsible for hiring and supervising crew, filming and editing reels, maintaining and outsourcing source material, and maintaining and storing production equipment.

BRIAN ACKLEY

Brian Ackley is a writer, director, producer, and editor for One Way Or Another Productions, a film company with more than 10 films in its current library. In 2008 Brian directed one feature film and co-produced two others. Brian has worked as a freelance videographer and editor for a number of independent clients. Since 2002, Brian has been associated with Beacon 43, an after school program administered by F.E.G.S. Far Rockaway in NY. His responsibilities have ranged from office management to video production instructor to activity specialist. Before that, Brian was the Office Manager for A.R. Real Estate Management based in Brooklyn.

Brian is the Executive Associate Director of PFI All-Sports. He supervises the design and maintenance of the infrastructure, accounting and payroll, and manages expenses and purchases.

AMY AFSARI

Amy Afsari is a former junior Olympic athlete. She competed for ten years in gymnastics throughout junior high and high school and has won numerous state, regional and national titles. She has demonstrated her love for athletics by participating in youth sports as a coach, mentor and program director for the Vanderbilt YMCA in New York City. Amy played a key role in business development and management of all aspects of recruiting operations for Algomod Technologies Corporation. She also acted as Executive Account Manager for Hyatt Leader Ltd. overseeing recruiting, sales, and advisory services. Amy has her degree in Broadcast Journalism.

Amy is the Public Relations Representative and Broadcasting Director of PFI All-Sports. She oversees all broadcasts, the maintenance and operation of the Mobile Processing and Printing Unit (van), communications with clients, coaches, and school officials, and event scheduling.
