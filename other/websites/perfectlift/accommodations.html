Luxurious Mountain Retreat - Trailside At Hunter Mountain

This luxurious newly constructed two bedroom, two and a half bath, duplex condo has over 1300 sq. ft. of living space. The condo is located directly on Hunter Mountain a few hundred feet from the slopes. The lower floor has a full kitchen, dining room, living room with an outdoor deck viewing the mountains, stone fireplace with 42"plasma television above the mantle, and half bath. In the master bedroom with a Queen bed, there is an outdoor deck with breathtaking views of the mountains, and a full bath. The second bedroom has a Queen bed and many windows for unbeatable mountain views. You will also have access to Club Hunter amenities just across the way, which include an outdoor pool, fitness center, game room. Come get away to this beautiful mountain retreat.
