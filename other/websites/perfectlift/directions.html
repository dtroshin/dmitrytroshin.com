Driving Directions

From Kingston/Phoenica

From the Kingstion traffic circle, take Rt 28 towards Pine Hill. Make a right into the town of Phoenicia. Make a right onto route 214. Make a left onto Ski Bowl Rd, Travel 3/4 mile look for a sign on left for Milan's Perfect Lift Resturant

Follow the arrow up the hill; 1/4 mile up the hill on the left hand side look for Club Hunter and Perfect Lift Resturant.

From Windham

Rt 296 to the end. Make a left onto 23A. Drive through the town of Hunter. Look for the sign for the main entrance to Hunter Mtn Ski/Mtn Biking/Orvis Ctr. Then a left onto Ski Bowl Rd. At the first stop sign, then a right onto the Hunter Highland Condo. Look for the Perfect Lift Restaurant Sign.

The restaurant will be on your left about 1/4 mile up the hill, it is part of Club Hunter

From Saugerties,NYS Thruway Exit 20

From the NYS Thruway (87) take Exit 20, the Saugerties Exit, which is at mile marker 100. Make a left after the toll both in Front of McDonalds and then the first right onto route 32.

Always follow the little green Hunter Mtn signs with a picture of a skier.

Take route 32 until you hit a light; bear left onto 32A. Go again to the next light and bear left onto 23A. Take 23A through the town of Haines Fall and Tannersville. Make a left onto 214 towards Phoenicia.

Go about 1/2 mile and make a right onto Ski Bowl Rd, the access Rd to Hunter Mountain Ski & Snowboard area. Look for the Perfect Lift Restaurant Sign on your left, it is inside of the Hunter Highlands & Trailside Condo area.

Albany - Est.Time: 1 Hour 10 Minutes

NY Thruway south to Exit 21 (catskill), turn left after exit and go to Rt. 9W (about 2 miles); take Rt. 9W south to Rt. 23A; take Rt. 23A west to Hunter Mountain. Go up the mountain Rd, through the village of Tannersville. Make a left onto 214 towards Phoenicia

Go about 1/2 mile and make a right onto Ski Bowl Rd, the access Rd to Hunter Mountain Ski & Snowboard area. Look for the Perfect Lift Restaurant Sign on your left, it is inside of the Hunter Highlands & Trailside Condo area.

New York City - Est. Time: 21/2 Hours

NY Thruway (I-87) north to Exit 20 (Saugerties). Follow directions about for Saugerties

Long Island - Est. Time: 21/2 - 5 Hours

Take the LIE or Rt. 25A to the Cross island Parkway to the Throgs Neck bridge. Then take the Cross Bronx Expressway (I-95) to the George Washington Bridge. Take the GW Bridge to Route 4 (3rd exit ramp). Continue on Rt. 4 heading northwest to Rt. 17 north to the NY Thruway, North to Exit 20 (Saugerties). Follow directions above from Saugerties.
